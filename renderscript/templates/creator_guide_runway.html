Edit picture first, then build audio in post

Keep visuals text-free and complete dialogue, SFX, and mix after picture lock.

Picture-first policy

  • No burned-in subtitles, captions, logos, or other text in the generated frame.
  • Reroll contaminated takes instead of trying to remove text downstream.
  • Layer voice, ambience, SFX, and music only after picture decisions are stable.
